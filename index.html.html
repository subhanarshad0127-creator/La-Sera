Our Story

A Taste of Italia
in Every Bite

Since 1987, La Sera has been Toronto's most beloved destination for authentic Italian cuisine. Born from the recipes of Nonna Rosa in Napoli, our kitchen has remained true to tradition — slow sauces, hand-rolled pasta, and wood-fired perfection.

Every evening at La Sera is an experience — candlelit tables, the aroma of fresh basil and truffle, and the warmth of Italian hospitality that makes you feel like family the moment you walk through our doors.
